PICTURE

As with Green’s theorem, if you traverse the boundary with your head pointing in the positive direction, the surface will be on your left. Here are a couple of other ways to think about this:

  • The boundary is oriented counterclockwise if you view it from the positive side.
  • The boundary follows the right hand rule with the orientation of the surface: if you point your right thumb in the direction of the normal vector, your fingers curl in the direction of the orientation on the boundary.
